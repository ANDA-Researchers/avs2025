Seminar - Machine Learning for Autonomous Vehicles

Welcome to the Machine Learning Seminar for Autonomous Vehicles by the ANDA Lab at Soongsil University. This course introduces the core technologies behind self-driving cars, with a focus on computer vision and machine learning. Students will explore key concepts and gain hands-on experience using real-world sensor and driving data to understand how autonomous systems perceive their environment.

Seminar Organization


Time: Every Tuesday at 4:00 PM

Duration: Starting from 24 June 2025, held weekly for 8 weeks

Location: Hyungnam Engineering Building

Language: English

Organizer: ANDA Lab

  • Week 1: Welcome and Introduction
  • Week 2: Python Programming
  • Week 3: Numpy & Matplotlib
  • Week 4: Machine Learning & Scikit-Learn
  • Week 5: Project I
  • Week 6: Deep Learning with Pytorch
  • Week 7: Deep Learning for 3D Data
  • Week 8: Project II
